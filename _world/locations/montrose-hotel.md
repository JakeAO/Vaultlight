**Type:** Landmark

## Overview
The Montrose Hotel is a historic, well-kept refuge a short walk from The Penumbra. It serves as the preferred rest stop for visiting high rollers and quiet operators, offering immaculate service and a culture of discretion that keeps stories contained.

## Atmosphere
- **Lighting / Weather:** Warm, amber lighting that softens the rain outside and keeps the lobby glowing late into the night.
- **Sound / Mood:** Low voices, soft piano, and the hush of thick carpets; calm, poised, and watchful.
- **Textures / Architecture:** Worn-but-cared-for rugs, ancient leather chairs, gold trim, and rich scarlets just shy of ostentatious.

## Notable Locations Within
### Scarlet Lobby
A gilded, scarlet-toned reception hall where guests are greeted by name and problems vanish behind a velvet rope.

### The Montrose Suites
Long-term rooms with old-world furnishings, sound-dampening walls, and a service call system that never asks questions.

### Service Corridors
Staff-only routes and delivery lifts that connect the hotel to nearby alleys, perfect for unobserved comings and goings.
