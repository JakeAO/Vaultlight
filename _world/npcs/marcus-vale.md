# Marcus Vale

**Role:** Fixer / Job Broker

## Description

Marcus Vale is a well-connected information broker who operates from a smoke-filled office above a jazz club in the Meridian District. Always impeccably dressed in a three-piece suit despite the perpetual gloom, he has a reputation for putting together crews for jobs others won't touch.

## Appearance

- Late 40s, silver-streaked hair slicked back
- Sharp grey suit with burgundy tie
- Gold cufflinks that catch the neon light
- Permanent cigarette holder
- Eyes that seem to see through deception

## Known Information

- **Connections:** Has contacts throughout the city's underworld
- **Motivation:** Unclear—money seems secondary to the thrill of impossible jobs
- **Reputation:** Never betrays a crew, but always has an angle
- **Warning:** Those who cross him tend to disappear

## Relationships

- **The Crew:** Assembled them for the Vaultlight job
- **The Umbral Syndicate:** Rumored connections, unconfirmed
- **Police:** Detective Winters seems to be watching him

## Quotes

> "The Vaultlight isn't just a vault, my friends. It's a question. Are you ready for the answer?"

> "Everyone in this city owes someone. The trick is knowing who."
