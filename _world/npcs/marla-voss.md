**Role:** Casino Floor Manager

## Description
Marla Voss runs the Penumbra's public floor, balancing glamour with control. She is polite, surgical, and ruthless about the house rules, and she can make problems disappear without ever raising her voice.

## Appearance
- Black velvet jacket with mirrored cufflinks
- A small headset hidden beneath her hair
- A scar on her knuckles from an old brawl

## Known Information
- **Location:** [The Penumbra]({{ '/world/locations/the-penumbra/' | relative_url }}), main floor and private rooms
- **Connections:** Penumbra security, [Iron District]({{ '/world/locations/iron-district/' | relative_url }}) fixers, house gamblers

## Aspects
- ***"Velvet Glove, Iron Rule"***
- ***"Every Game Is Watched"***
