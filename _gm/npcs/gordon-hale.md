**Role:** Concierge / Logistics Broker

## Description
Gordon Hale is the Montrose's public face of service and the back channel for everything else. He arranges transport, alibis, and last-minute amenities, always with a smile that never reveals how many problems he just solved.

## Appearance
- Silver hair, perfectly combed
- Polished shoes and a gold lapel chain
- Quiet, appraising eyes that miss nothing

## Known Information
- **Location:** Montrose Hotel, concierge desk and suites
- **Connections:** Cab companies, shipping clerks, black-market couriers
- **Goals:** Expand his favor network and keep the Montrose indispensable
- **Threat Level:** Low direct danger, high leverage

## Relationships
Gordon trades information with Iron District dock supervisors and keeps a standing tab with Penumbra bartenders.

## Aspects
- ***"Anything, For the Right Favor"***
- ***"Smile That Hides the Leverage"***
- ***"Network of Quiet Debts"***
