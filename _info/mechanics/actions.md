# Actions in FATE

In FATE Condensed, there are four types of actions you can take:

## Overcome

Use Overcome to get past obstacles or achieve goals using a relevant skill.

**Roll:** Skill + 4dF vs. Target difficulty
**Success:** You achieve your goal
**Failure:** You fail, or succeed at a cost

**Example:** Picking a lock (Burglary), climbing a fence (Athletics), researching archives (Lore)

## Create an Advantage

Create or discover aspects that you or allies can invoke for bonuses.

**Roll:** Skill + 4dF vs. Opposition
**Success:** Create aspect with one free invoke
**Success with Style:** Create aspect with two free invokes

**Example:** "Distracted Guards", "Weak Structural Point", "Knows the Secret"

## Attack

Attempt to harm an opponent or overcome their defenses.

**Roll:** Attack skill + 4dF vs. Defend skill + 4dF
**Success:** Deal stress equal to shifts
**Success with Style:** Deal stress + boost

**Example:** Shooting (Shoot), punching (Fight), psychological warfare (Provoke)

## Defend

React to another character's action targeting you.

**Roll:** Defend skill + 4dF vs. Attack/Overcome roll
**Success:** You avoid the action's effects
**Failure:** You suffer the consequences

**Example:** Dodging (Athletics), blocking (Fight), resisting interrogation (Will)
